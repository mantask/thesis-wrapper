USVIS for Health-Care

A specialised business management system for health-care centres. It simplifies appointments scheduling, staff assignments, billing, and customer management; and integrates with national and in-house information systems.

A-GAIN has built the product from the ground up with the help of domain experts at a private health-care centre. We are proud that USVIS has become the core of business operations for our clients.

This is our 'flagship' product.

Pocket TV Guide

A TV.LT mobile application enables you to see what is on Lithuanian television channels.

It is the second most popular Lithuanian mobile app (source: Omnitel.lt), nominated as a mobile app of the year during the biggest internet conference in the Baltic states LOGIN 2011.

A-GAIN has led the design and development effort of a new content delivery platform for our partners at TV.LT. We are continuing to support Android version.

Our Approach

Strong Values. Holistic view. Systematic approach. Honesty. Professional growth.

Business Insight. When you come to us with your idea or problem, first we try to get into the essence of your business. Is it economically feasible to build custom software? What to build? What integration strategy to take? What revenue model to choose? Is mobile a good choice? How to organize communication of your products / services on the Internet?

Agile Process. We stay flexible and adjust to the changing requirements over the course of the product development. We have adopted proven agile practices that empower pragmatic collaboration and strengthen communication. Let us help you to discover the flexible and cost-effective way of software development.

Users First. When creating products we focus on the end-user. Usefulness, convenience, and aesthetics are three main goals, which we aim to pursue with our products / services. We achieve these goals by a user-centric approach and direct dialog with the future users of the system.

Recognized Expertise. Our team of certified professionals has a solid foundation of technical and management skills. We regularly participate in professional development events and local user groups.

  • Sun Certified Java Programmer
  • Zend Certified Engineer PHP 5.3
  • Scrum User Group Lithuania

Proven Platform. We build our web-based solutions on a standards-based architecture—open source LAMP stack. Over the years of information systems development we have built a reusable component library, that allows a rapid custom solution implementation.
